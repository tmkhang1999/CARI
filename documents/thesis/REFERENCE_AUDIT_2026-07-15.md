## Textual Corrections Triggered by the Audit

- Chapter 4 now cites InteriorVerse instead of leaving the training corpus
  unsupported.
- MID and MID Intrinsics are no longer conflated: Murmann et al. provide the
  capture dataset; Careaga and Aksoy's later tooling provides the dense
  pseudo-albedo used here.
- Literature values remain separated from locally rerun results because citation
  correctness does not make differing evaluation protocols directly comparable.
