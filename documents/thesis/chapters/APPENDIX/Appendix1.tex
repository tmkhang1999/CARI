\section{ARAP Per-Scene Constancy}

\tabref{tab:arap_perscene} reports $C_{\text{arap}}$ and $\text{Cast}_{\text{RMS}}$
for every scene in the ARAP dataset separately.
We include results for our full model and Marigold-App.
Scenes flagged as degenerate (those containing conference-room glass, extreme
specular highlights, or other physical edge cases that produce near-infinite colour
spread) are excluded from the aggregate numbers reported in \chapref{ch:experiments};
they are listed at the end of the table with a $\dagger$ marker.
